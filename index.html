A Court of Thorns and Roses

By

Sarah J. Mass

Goodreads Rating

4.18 Stars

MUST READ

If you’re craving a book that combines romance, action, and breathtaking fantasy, then this irresistible story will hooks you from the very first page, pulling you into a world brimming with magic, danger, and heart-stopping romance.

CHARACTERS

The protagonist, Feyre, is a fierce and relatable heroine, and her journey from survival to empowerment is deeply compelling.

Rhysand, the enigmatic and swoon-worthy High Lord, is everything you’d want in a book boyfriend.

THE WORLD

he relationships between the characters are rich with tension, wit, and emotion, making you root for them with every page.

And the world-building? Stunning. From the beautiful courts of Prythian to its darker corners, you’ll be lost in its lore and longing for more.


ACOTAR is a Epic, Romance and Action.

A Court of Thorns and Roses captivated me with its richly layered characters and immersive storytelling. Feyre, the resilient and determined protagonist, draws readers in with her courage and vulnerability. Rhysand, with his complex blend of darkness and tenderness, is impossible not to adore. The dynamic relationships, particularly the deep bond between Feyre and Rhysand, are beautifully explored, adding emotional depth.

The story itself is a masterful blend of romance, action, and fantasy, set in a world so vividly crafted it feels real. Each twist kept me on edge, making the series an unforgettable journey of love, sacrifice, and self-discovery.
